About Ixia

Ixia, the Archlight Outlaw, is a highly effective marksman in Mobile Legends, known for her ability to deal devastating damage from a distance. A master of both finishers and burst damage, she thrives in the gold lane, capable of quickly eliminating enemies in the late game. Her precision and agility make her a formidable foe in team fights, especially when properly equipped with the right gear.

Abilities

Ability 1: Archlight Strike

Ixia fires a powerful shot at a target, dealing damage based on the enemy's health. This ability allows her to execute low-health enemies and finish off fleeing foes.

Ability 2: Outlaw's Fury

Ixia enhances her attack speed and movement speed, allowing her to quickly reposition during team fights and deal consistent damage over time.

Ultimate: Archlight Barrage

Ixia unleashes a barrage of energy arrows in a cone, dealing massive physical damage to enemies in the area. This ultimate ability is perfect for both team fights and finishing off high-health enemies.

Easy combos to learn

During the laning phase, be aggressive and focus on activating your passive ability against the enemy marksman as frequently as possible.

Once you reach level four, wait for your teammates to initiate a team fight before activating your ultimate ability, Full Barrage.

Itâ€™s important to note that Ixia is vulnerable while using her ultimate, so ensure that you have Flicker available in case an enemy hero attempts to burst you down.

The best combo in team fights is to first activate Full Barrage, followed by Dual Beam and Star Helix. Use them on priority targets such as the marksman or jungler. After that, use your auto attack to finish the job.

By using your skills on important targets first, they will be the first ones to receive the Siphon Starlium damage and will be the first to perish in the team fight.

The key strategy is to always follow up each skill with a basic attack to maximize the activation of Siphon Starlium.

Avoid using Full Barrage when your other two skills are on cooldown, as their cooldowns do not reset once the ultimate is activated.
