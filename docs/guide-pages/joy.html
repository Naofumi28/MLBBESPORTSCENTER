About Joy

Joy is a powerful Mage in Mobile Legends with strong burst damage and poke abilities. She excels at dealing damage from a distance and has great mobility for engaging in mid-lane fights. She has high burst damage, which can quickly eliminate enemies if used properly.

Abilities

Ability 1: Joyful Burst

Joy launches a burst of magic energy in a target direction, dealing magic damage to all enemies in its path. This ability is perfect for poking enemies from a safe distance and harassing them during laning phase.

Ability 2: Miracle Dash

Joy dashes forward, dealing magic damage to all enemies she passes through. She can use this to quickly close gaps or escape dangerous situations. The dash grants a brief shield for extra survivability.

Ultimate: Flash of Miracle

Joy unleashes her ultimate ability, dealing massive magic damage to all nearby enemies and significantly reducing their movement speed. This ability is excellent for team fights and can secure kills with its powerful area-of-effect damage.

Battle spell recommendation

Credit: One Esports

When playing Joy in the EXP lane or midlane, choosing Vengeance as your battle spell is a smart decision, especially because she is relatively easy to burst down. Vengeance provides you with the means to stay alive for a longer duration and inflict damage on foes who target you while you build up your rhythm.

How to know the best type of Retribution for you in Mobile Legends

Retribution is another good battle spell for her since she can be played in the jungle. Upgrade it to Ice Retribution to get a movement speed debuff to enemy heroes, which works well with her mobility skills. It can also act as an escape tool whenever you fail to hit your second skill.
