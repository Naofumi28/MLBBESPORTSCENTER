What is MPL MY Season 14

Credit: ONE Esports

MPL MY Season 14 is Malaysia's official Mobile Legends tournament, organized by Moonton Games.

10 teams, two of which came from the qualifier, play in the regular season for six weeks to determine who advances to the playoffs.

Credit: ONE Esports

The grand finalists of this tournament will be selected for the M6 World Championship, which will be held later this year on home soil.

MPL MY Season 14 format

The regular season follows a single-round-robin, best-of-three format. The top eight teams then proceed to the playoffs, while the bottom two get relegated.
