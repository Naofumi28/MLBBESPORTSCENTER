About Cici

Cici, the Buoyant Performer, is a fighter known for her ability to deal damage and regenerate health simultaneously. She thrives in the mid lane where she can dominate opponents with her versatile skills. Cici's skills allow her to heal quickly while dealing consistent physical damage, making her a dangerous opponent in prolonged fights.

Abilities

Ability 1: Buoyant Surge

Cici surges forward with a burst of energy, dealing physical damage to enemies in her path and healing herself for a portion of the damage dealt. This ability allows her to stay in the fight longer, dealing damage while sustaining her health.

Ability 2: Flowing Strike

Cici performs a quick strike, dealing damage to enemies around her and temporarily increasing her attack speed. This ability boosts her effectiveness in skirmishes and helps her output more damage over time.

Ultimate: Harmony Burst

Cici unleashes a burst of harmonious energy, healing herself and allies in the vicinity while dealing damage to enemies in the area. This ultimate serves as both a support and damage-dealing skill, making her a versatile hero in team fights.
