About Nolan

Nolan, the Cosmic Wayfinder, is an assassin known for his incredible burst damage and mobility. Mastering the art of chasing enemies with precision, Nolan excels at eliminating high-priority targets and quickly escaping danger. His presence in the jungle is felt as he swiftly strikes down foes, utilizing his energy resource and quick reflexes to remain a constant threat in the game.

Abilities

Ability 1: Cosmic Dash

Nolan dashes a short distance in the targeted direction, dealing physical damage to enemies he passes through. This ability allows him to chase enemies or escape danger quickly.

Ability 2: Stellar Barrage

Nolan fires a series of cosmic projectiles in a targeted direction, dealing burst damage to all enemies hit. This ability excels at dealing damage in quick bursts.

Ultimate: Cosmic Collapse

Nolan calls down a massive cosmic explosion, dealing significant damage to enemies in the area of effect. This ultimate can turn the tide of battle by eliminating multiple enemies at once.
