About Novaria

Novaria is a powerful Mage character in Mobile Legends, known as the "Star Rebel". She excels at burst damage and long-range poke attacks. With her immense magical power and strong crowd control abilities, she can dominate in team fights and help secure victories for her team. Novaria's abilities allow her to control the battlefield and deal devastating magic damage from a safe distance.

Abilities

Ability 1: Stellar Barrage

Novaria summons a burst of energy from the stars, dealing magic damage to all enemies in the area of effect. This ability can be used to clear waves and poke enemies from a distance, making her an excellent mid-lane mage.

Ability 2: Cosmic Shield

Novaria creates a cosmic barrier around herself or an ally, reducing incoming damage for a few seconds. This ability can be used strategically to protect allies or herself during critical moments of a fight.

Ultimate: Supernova

Novaria releases her ultimate power, summoning a massive explosion of cosmic energy that deals huge amounts of magic damage to all enemies in the area. It also stuns them for a short duration, making it perfect for initiating team fights or securing kills in the late game.
