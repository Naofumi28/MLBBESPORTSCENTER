With this win, Selangor Red Giants secure the lion's share of the US$100,000 prize pool and will represent Malaysia, alongside Team Vamos, at the M6 World Championship, which will be held on their home turf. The team was a force to reckon with in the regular season, dropping only a single series. They then stumbled against Todak in the playoffs and were sent to the lower bracket.

Credit: Selangor Red Giants Esports

They recovered, defeating Todak in a rematch during the lower bracket final to earn their place in the grand final and ultimately seize the championship.

Selangor Red Giants wins MPL MY Season 14 title in intense grand final showdown against Team Vamos

Selangor Red Giants started the series with a statement, securing a dominating victory in the opening game. Team Vamos, however, showed resilience, bouncing back to even the series at 1-1.

Despite the early setback, the defending champions proved their mettle. They powered through games three and four, with standout performances from jungler Muhammad Haqqullah "Sekys" Ahmad Shahrul Zaman and roamer Muhammad "Yums" Suhairi, whose MVP-worthy plays shifted the momentum in Selangor Red Giants' favor.

Game five saw Team Vamos implement a team-fight-heavy composition, featuring Hylos, Cecilion, and Suyou, aimed at countering Sekys' signature Alpha pick alongside their formidable Mathilda–Ixia combo.
