Tournament Format

The tournament will feature a group stage followed by playoffs. Teams will be divided into four groups of four teams each, playing in a double-elimination format.

The top two teams from each group will advance to the upper bracket of playoffs, while the bottom two teams will start from the lower bracket.

Schedule and Venue

The M6 World Championship will take place from November 28 to December 15, 2024, at the Axiata Arena in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

This marks the first time Malaysia will host the prestigious Mobile Legends world championship event.
