About Arlott

Arlott is a versatile Fighter/Assassin hero in Mobile Legends, known as the "Lone Lancer." He excels in burst damage and changing the flow of the battle with his powerful skills. With his unique ability to close gaps quickly and deal heavy physical damage, Arlott is a fearsome presence on the battlefield, especially in the EXP lane.

Abilities

Ability 1: Lancer's Charge

Arlott charges forward, dealing physical damage to all enemies in his path and knocking them back. This ability is ideal for closing gaps and dealing initial damage to enemies, making it a powerful tool for initiating fights.

Ability 2: Lancer's Wrath

Arlott swings his weapon in a wide arc, dealing physical damage to enemies around him. This ability also provides bonus movement speed for a short duration, helping Arlott chase down enemies or escape dangerous situations.

Ultimate: Wrath of the Lone Lancer

Arlott unleashes his ultimate power, dealing massive physical damage to all enemies in a targeted area and stunning them for a short duration. This ability is ideal for dealing a decisive blow during team fights or securing kills in the late game.
